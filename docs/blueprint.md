# **App Name**: Local Eats Hub

## Core Features:

- Food Discovery: Browse local homemade food listings by category, cuisine, or dietary restrictions.
- Food Listing: List available dishes with descriptions, prices, pickup/drop-off options, and photos.
- Order Management: Secure ordering and scheduling system for consumers to request and arrange food pickup/drop-off.
- User Profiles: Profiles for consumers and providers.
- AI-Powered Tag Suggestions: Suggest appropriate tags for new dishes using a tool informed by the image of the dish, helping providers categorize and consumers to find the listing easily.
- Rating and Review System: Ability for consumers to rate and review food and providers to build trust and accountability.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm orange (#FF9933) to evoke feelings of home-cooked meals and comfort.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), a desaturated variant of the primary, for a neutral and inviting backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted green (#9ACD32), an analogous color, symbolizing freshness and natural ingredients.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for readability and a modern yet warm feel.
- Use clear, recognizable icons to represent different cuisines, dietary restrictions, and features.
- Clean and intuitive layout with high-quality food photography to entice users.
- Subtle animations for transitions and feedback to enhance user engagement.
